Funny Storry

Three weeks ago I had the opportunity to visit a dentist at a government hospital in Bangkok. Of course, there were a lot of people, and I had to wait in line for more than two hours. While everyone stood in a orderly line But suddenly, there was a human uncle who didn't know who was standing in line and walking with selfish eyes. Until he reaches straight to me I didn't hesitate, hitting that uncle's face with full strength. The nurse rushed to stop me. I told the nurse that other patients would like to see the doctor quickly. But how do they have a conscience? The nurse rushed back to me immediately. "Bastard! It's where you punch, doctor. "
